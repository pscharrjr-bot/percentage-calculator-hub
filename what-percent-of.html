How this calculator works

To find what percent one value is of another, divide the part by the whole and multiply by 100. This calculator handles that instantly.

Formula: (part ÷ whole) × 100

Example

If you want to know what percent 25 is of 80:

(25 ÷ 80) × 100 = 31.25%

So 25 is 31.25% of 80.

Frequently asked questions

How do I find what percent one number is of another?
Divide the part by the whole, then multiply by 100.
What is 30 out of 120 as a percentage?
30 out of 120 is 25%.
Why can’t the whole be zero?
Because dividing by zero is not valid, so the calculator needs a whole value greater than or less than zero.
