How this calculator works

To find a percentage of a number, multiply the number by the percentage and divide by 100. This calculator does that instantly.

Formula: (percentage ÷ 100) × number

Example

If you want to know what 15% of 240 is:

(15 ÷ 100) × 240 = 36

So 15% of 240 is 36.
